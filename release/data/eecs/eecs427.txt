Title: EECS427

Name:  VLSI Design I

Credits: 4

Description: Design techniques for rapid implementations of very large-scale integrated (VLSI) circuits, MOS technology and logic. Structured design. Design rules, layout procedures. Design aids: layout, design rule checking, logic, and circuit simulation. Timing. Testability. Architectures for VLSI. Projects to develop and lay out circuits.

Pre-requisites: EECS270 EECS312
